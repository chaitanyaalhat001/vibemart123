Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account and add money to your wallet to complete your purchase.

How does the wallet system work?

Our secure wallet system allows you to add money and use it for purchases. You can add funds using the wallet management section in your dashboard.

How can I become a vendor?

Click on "Become a Vendor" in the navigation menu, fill out the registration form, and start selling your products immediately after approval.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you'll receive a tracking ID. You can use this ID to track your order status in the "My Orders" section of your dashboard.
